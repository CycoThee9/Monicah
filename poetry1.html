Whispers of the Heart

Tender verses of romance and devotion

In Your Eyes, I Found Home

In the depths of your eyes, I found myself,
A reflection I never knew I needed,
Like a wanderer finding sanctuary,
Like starlight finding the darkest sky.

Your gaze holds centuries of kindness,
A warmth that melts the frost of doubt,
In those pools of endless wonder,
I discovered the meaning of home.

Not a place of walls and stone,
But a space where my heart belongs,
Where every beat echoes your name,
Where love is the only language spoken.

So let me stay in those beautiful eyes,
Forever lost, forever found,
In the whispers of your glance,
I found my eternal home.

~ A testament to recognition ~

The Sweetness of Your Name

Monicah—a melody on trembling lips,
A name that tastes like honey and starlight,
When I speak it, angels pause to listen,
The universe bends to its sweetness.

Three syllables that contain oceans,
Mountains of devotion, valleys of tenderness,
Your name is a symphony my soul plays,
On strings of love, in chords of forever.

I could whisper it a thousand times,
And each utterance would be a prayer,
A confession, a promise, a song,
Monicah, Monicah, Monicah—always.

For no word in any language,
Could capture your essence, your light,
Yet your name comes closest to perfection,
A poem in itself, written by fate.

~ A tribute to nomenclature ~

Stolen Moments

I collect stolen moments with you,
Like a miser counts precious gold,
A glance across a crowded room,
A hand that brushes against mine.

Each moment is a thread of gold,
Weaving through the tapestry of my days,
Creating patterns of joy and wonder,
A masterpiece only we can see.

Your laughter, a melody I hoard,
Your smile, an elixir I consume,
These stolen fragments of your presence,
Are riches beyond mortal measure.

Though time may be a thief,
Taking these moments away,
The feeling remains eternal,
Imprinted on my soul forever.

~ A celebration of cherished instants ~

You Are the Reason

You are the reason I believe in magic,
In a world that often feels mundane,
In the possibility of perfect moments,
In the existence of true connection.

You are the reason my heart dares to dream,
To hope for futures yet unwritten,
To embrace vulnerability without fear,
To love without reservation or doubt.

Every morning I wake, grateful,
For a world that contains your presence,
Every sunset I pause to give thanks,
For the gift of knowing your soul.

So if ever you wonder your worth,
Look into the eyes of those who love you,
See the difference you have made,
You are the reason—always, forever, completely.

~ A declaration of purpose ~
